Cow and Milk Management

Website Guide

What can you do in this website.

Cow Management

The Cow Management section is where you can efficiently organize and track information about your cattle. Easily add, edit, and remove cow records, including details such as name, breed, date of birth

Medical Consultations

The Medical Consultations feature enables you to keep comprehensive health records for your cows. Record details of veterinary visits, diagnoses, and any other relevant medical information.

Cow births Registrations

In the Cow Births section, you can document the births of new calves on your farm. Record essential information such as the mother's ID and date of birth.

Milk Production Management

Milk Production allows you to monitor and analyze the milk output of your cows. Record daily milk quantities along with the date, making it easy to identify trends and patterns in production.
